About Me:

I'm working as a Post-Doctoral Fellow, at Intelligent User Interface Lab (IUI-Lab), Dept.of Computer and Information Science, Seikei University,Tokyo, Japan.
I completed my Ph.D under the supervision of Dr. Deepu Vijayasenan at Electronics and Communication Engineering department, National Institute of Technology Karnataka - Surathkal, Mangalore, India, since December 2013.

During my PhD, I mainly focuses on Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) techniques in Speaker Profiling - estimating physical parameters from short-duration speech utterances with an emphasis on forensics and e-commerce applications. The availability of recorded speech data will be less in hoax/threatening calls in forensic scenarios. However, in such cases, the available speech resources may not be monolingual and do not depend on the phone level transcriptions. In my research, a speaker profiling system is developed with a short duration (2-3s) of speech data in a multilingual setting without transcription alignments. My goal is to advance machine learning/deep learning techniques that lead to an improved understanding of speaker analytics with low resources.


Prior to that, I did my Master of Technology in Digital Electronics and Communication System from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapur (JNTUA)(2011-2013), Andhra Pradesh and Bachelor's degree from JNTUA (2007-2011), Andhra Pradesh,India.

Research Interests:
Speech Signal Processing, Speaker Profiling and Characterization, Deep Learning, Machine Learning and Pattern Recognition.
